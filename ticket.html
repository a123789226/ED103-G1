Aqua Wonderland is a marine life conservation organization. You can see the marine life that is being restored in the pavilion. There are four areas in our venue, namely Dolphin House, Whale House, Seal House and Turtle House.
The admission time of Aqua Wonderland is from 9 am to 18 pm. Tickets are divided into adult tickets (ages 10 to 65 years old), children tickets (ages under 10 years old) and elder tickets (ages over 65 years old). , We also have the overnight activity. Welcome to book in advance!
