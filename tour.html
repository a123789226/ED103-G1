Dolphin Aquarium

This is the Dolphin Aquarium, a two-story building with a slanted roof, which can keep the museum warm in winter and cool in summer in hot summer. At present, the museum houses about 30 dolphins. Under the careful care of the staff, they gradually recover their health and vitality. Here, you can admire the dolphins up close and feel their cuteness and wonder.

Whale Aquarium

This is the whale Aquarium, the exterior building is quite pleasing pink. The first floor is the main exhibition area, where you can interact with whales. There is a large square on the second floor, where people can take a break and enjoy the beautiful scenery. On the top floor is a sky garden with full of green trees. welcome you to find out more!

Seal Aquarium

This is the Seal Aquarium, a long cube building that is marvellous. As soon as you enter the hall, first of all, you will see a pair of lovely statues of mother and child seals welcoming you at the door. It is worth mentioning that there is a huge seal-shaped slide in the museum. You must come and have a look!

Turtle Aquarium

This is the Turtle Aquarium. The building itself is elevated to make the room more ventilated. Large pieces of glass allow sunlight to spill into the room naturally, which is environmentally friendly, it allows you to overlook the beautiful scenery. As a reminder, please lower the volume when you see turtles , so as not to scare them.
